# 📊 Market Segmentation & Brand Positioning Analytics

This project applies marketing analytics techniques to two real-world cases:

1. ISBM Market Segmentation  
2. Positioning the Infiniti G20  

Methods used include:

- Hierarchical Cluster Analysis  
- Discriminant Analysis  
- Perceptual Mapping (MDS)  
- Segment Targeting & Repositioning Strategy  

---

# 1️⃣ ISBM – Market Segmentation

## 📌 Objective

Identify distinct market segments and recommend targeting strategies for retention and acquisition.

---

## 🔎 Segmentation Results

A **2-cluster solution** was selected based on the dendrogram structure and linkage distance jump.

### Segment 1: Network Builders (≈40%)

**Profile:**
- Value networking and consulting
- Prefer practical tools (business templates)
- Larger firms with higher revenue
- More likely to have a CMO
- Overrepresented in chemical/product industries
- Located closer to State College

**Strategic Role:**
Primary target for retention campaigns

---

### Segment 2: Global Innovators (≈60%)

**Profile:**
- Value global exposure and research
- Prefer advanced courses and recruiting services
- More hi-tech and service firms
- Smaller revenue and marketing teams
- Geographically more dispersed

**Strategic Role:**
Primary target for acquisition campaigns

---

## 📊 Discriminant Analysis Insights

Key differentiators between segments:

- Industry type (Chemical vs Hi-Tech/Services)
- Distance from campus
- Revenue size
- Marketing team size
- Presence of CMO

This validates the robustness of the 2-segment solution.

---

## 🎯 Targeting Recommendation

- Retain: Network Builders (strong alignment with core offerings)
- Acquire: Global Innovators (leverage global programs & recruiting services)

---

# 2️⃣ Infiniti G20 – Brand Positioning Analysis

## 📌 Objective

Understand brand perception and identify optimal repositioning strategy using perceptual mapping.

---

## 📊 Dimensional Analysis

The first 2 dimensions explain **70.3%** of variance.  
Adding the third dimension increases explanation to **84%**.

### Key Dimensions Identified:

1️⃣ Image & Prestige  
   (Attractive, Successful, Prestige)

2️⃣ Roominess & Service  
   (Roomy, Easy Service, Avant-Garde)

3️⃣ Economic Value & Interest  
   (Economical, Poor Value, Interesting)

---

## 👥 Market Segments

### Segment I – Well Positioned
- Strong preference for prestige and image
- Values comfort and quietness
- Highest alignment with Infiniti G20

### Segment II – Possible to Extend
- Values functionality and roominess
- Moderate alignment
- Opportunity for strategic repositioning

### Segment III – Hard to Extend
- Focuses on economy and uniqueness
- Weak alignment with G20

---

## 🎯 Target Market Recommendation

Primary Target: **Segment I**

Secondary Target: **Segment II**

Avoid heavy investment in Segment III (requires major repositioning).

---

## 🚗 Repositioning Strategy

### For Segment I:
- Emphasize luxury, prestige, and refined image
- Reinforce quiet comfort and reliability
- Use premium branding and experiential marketing

### For Segment II:
- Highlight roominess and convenience
- Promote ease of service
- Emphasize practical luxury at strong value

---

## 📢 Marketing Program

Segment I:
- Luxury media placements
- Influencer endorsements
- Premium showroom experiences

Segment II:
- Digital campaigns focused on functionality
- Value-driven messaging
- Lifestyle positioning
